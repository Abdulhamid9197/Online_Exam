The Dangers of Sugar

Sugar is a common ingredient in many foods and drinks, but consuming too much can have serious health consequences.

Health Risks of Excessive Sugar Intake

  • Obesity and Weight Gain
  • Type 2 Diabetes
  • Heart Disease
  • Stroke
  • Fatty Liver Disease
  • Tooth Decay
  • Certain Cancers

Tips for Reducing Sugar Intake

Tip Description
Read Food Labels Be mindful of added sugars in processed foods.
Limit Sugary Drinks Choose water, unsweetened tea, or black coffee instead.
Eat Whole Foods
Cook More Meals at Home You have more control over ingredients.
Choose Natural Sweeteners Use fruits, dates, or honey in moderation.
